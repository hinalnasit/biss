Features

YOUR WEBSITE FEATURES INCLUDE

Check out all the great features that are included on your website! SimpleSite provides everything you need to create a beautiful and professional website.

Design, backgrounds and colours

A SimpleSite website lets you choose between hundreds of unique designs. Choose between our many different templates, colours and backgrounds – or choose a ready-made design.

Personal Domain Name

You can choose your own unique domain name. A domain name will make your website more personal and easier to find on search engines like Google. Having your own domain name will also help strengthen your brand.

Your own online store

Your SimpleSite website also includes your own professional online store that enables you to offer online payments. Regardless of whether you have your own company or just want to sell a few things now and then, our online store is perfect for you.

24/7 Customer Service

Our customer service is ready to help you when you have questions – or check out our frequently asked questions in our customer service section.

Photo Album

Showcase your photos in a beautiful online photo album. Choose between various slideshow themes, layouts and photo transitions.

Video

Make your website more dynamic by using our video player. You can add videos from YouTube or Vimeo – or upload your own videos.

Contact Page

The Contact Page makes it easy for your visitors to send private messages directly to you.

Map

Add a map on your website to show visitors where you are located.

Email addresses

Included with each domain purchase are 5 personal email addresses.You could for example choose yourfirstname@domainname.com.
